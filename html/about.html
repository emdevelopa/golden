Meet Mike Anyakudo - CEO, Goldenspiro Limited.

Mike Anyakudo is an international education consultant of international repute. Through his network of partners, he has helped several students achieve their goals of continuing their education in top schools overseas.

He is a member / associate member of several international organizations including;

  • Student Affairs Administrators in Higher Education (NASPA), USA.
  • International Consultants for Education and Fairs (ICEF), USA.
  • European Association for International Education (EAIE), Amsterdam, Netherlands.
  • The Forum on Education Abroad, USA.
  • Diversity Abroad, USA.
